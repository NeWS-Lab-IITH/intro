# Welcome to NeWS Lab

## Introduction
The Networked Wireless Systems (NeWS) laboratory at IIT Hyderabad engages in a wide range of experimental and theoretical research in Wireless networks, Network softwarization, Network security, IoT, and ICT for societal development.

## Mission
To advance the state-of-the-art in wireless networking through innovative research and to develop solutions for next-generation wireless systems.

## Vision
To be a world-class research lab contributing to the development of future wireless technologies and training the next generation of researchers.
